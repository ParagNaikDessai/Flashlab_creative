Programme Overview

Not Your Typical
Internship

At Flashlab Creative, interns don't fetch coffee or do busywork. From day one, you'll be embedded in a live team working on real client campaigns — designing, coding, strategizing, and creating alongside our senior professionals.

Our 3 to 6 month programme gives you hands-on experience, direct mentorship from industry veterans, and a portfolio you can actually be proud of. Top performers get fast-tracked to full-time roles.

Apply for Internship
Areas of Learning

What You'll Learn

Graphic Design

Assist in creating visuals for social media, branding, print, and digital campaigns using Adobe Creative Suite.

Video & Animation

Support the production team with editing, motion graphics, reel creation, and content production workflows.

Web Development

Contribute to web projects, landing pages, and app builds under the guidance of our senior development team.

Social Media

Help manage client social accounts, plan content calendars, write captions, and analyse engagement metrics.

SEO & Marketing

Learn keyword research, on-page optimisation, Google Analytics, and reporting tasks alongside our growth team.

Photography & Production

Join shoots, learn the production process, assist with behind-the-scenes tasks, and build your creative eye.

Intern Benefits

What You Get

Certificate & LOR

Receive a professional Certificate of Completion and a personalised Letter of Recommendation upon successfully finishing the programme.

Stipend

Compensation based on your skillset, department, and performance. We believe good work deserves fair recognition from day one.

Fast-Track to Full Time

Top-performing interns are offered full-time positions. Many of our current team members started exactly where you are now.
