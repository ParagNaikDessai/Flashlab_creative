Our Policies

Standards We Live By

Working Hours

Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST. We believe in focused, productive work during office hours so you can enjoy your evenings and Sundays fully.

Leave Policy

Paid casual leaves, sick leaves, and all public holidays observed. We understand life happens and ensure our team has the flexibility they need.

Code of Conduct

Professional behaviour, mutual respect, and inclusion are non-negotiable. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment, discrimination, and toxicity.

Compensation

Salaries are structured around your skillset, creativity, mindset, and experience. We offer competitive pay with clear structures — no guesswork involved.

Growth & Reviews

Quarterly performance reviews with honest, constructive feedback. Promotions and raises are merit-based — hard work and results always get recognised here.

Equal Opportunity

We are an equal opportunity employer. Hiring, promotions, and all decisions are based on merit, skill, and attitude — never on gender, background, or identity.

Workplace Culture

Beyond The Policies

Open Communication

Direct access to leadership. Ideas and concerns are heard through regular team meetings, 1-on-1 check-ins, and an always-open-door culture.

Team Events & Celebrations

Regular outings, birthday celebrations, festival parties, and team-building activities throughout the year. Work hard, celebrate harder.

Learning & Development

We invest in your growth through workshops, skill-sharing sessions, and access to premium tools and resources for continuous professional development.

Have Questions About Our Policies?

Reach out to us anytime. We're happy to clarify anything before you join the team.
